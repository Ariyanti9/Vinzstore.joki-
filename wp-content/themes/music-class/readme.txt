== Description ==

As you start looking out for free WordPress theme to launch your Music Class online, you’ll find multiple-use themes and niche themes. The Music Class WordPress theme we’ve designed caters to niche audience, comprising professionals giving music lessons to individuals and batches of students, and those who conduct musical events. If you’re looking to take your music class online to improve your demand, registration, visibility, look no further as the platform we’ve created offers a perfect layout to display your profession in the most relevant setting! 
The well-chosen layout & design of our Music Class WordPress theme offers your initiative an excellent start! As a musician, music teacher, or band owner, finding the right WordPress theme plays a critical role in building or breaking your effort. In addition to striking design, you also ought to look for a theme that offers essential features such as audio players, section that will enable you to share event planning information, create a good navigation experience for your visitor, and more.
Whether you’re preparing to promote your upcoming gig, or you’re organizing music class for a particular age group, or to teach particular type of songs or music through a short duration music class, you want to spread a word about giving music class individually, or you simply want to share your favorite music links with your community Music Class WordPress theme will not fall short of your expectations. Minimalistic design, easy to manage interface, responsive design, fast to load, visually attractive, the music class WordPress theme is for people who want to stay in the business of music for long term. The theme is designed to take your work a long way and make your journey fulfilling as well as successful!


== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
